# Highlights / Key Points

- Public OpenNeuro EEG datasets were used to pressure-test resting and task EEG findings in TBI.
- The acute mTBI versus control D1 result did not survive broad artifact-controlled FDR after ptp95 trimming.
- D3 eyes-closed alpha/IAF did not rescue the acute signal.
- D2 showed only a weak DPX cue-baseline trace; task-average and integrated task models did not converge.
- The manuscript is a transparent reproducibility/falsification report, with chronic TBI separate and ds003490 comparator-only.
